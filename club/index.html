Santa Clara Diving Club

a California non-profit public benefit club.

At the Santa Clara Diving Club, we take pride in offering the sport of diving to all skill levels. Our programs include beginning recreational classes all the way through Senior National Finalists and everything in between. We believe that, through sports, young athletes can learn valuable lifelong lessons.
