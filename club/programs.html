Junior Olympic (JO) Team

  This group is the top tier of our “Junior Olympic” travel team. The athletes in this group train 6 days per week and attend Local, Regional, Zone, National, and, if qualified, International competitions. These athletes are the “ambassadors” of our team. This group is made up of proven competitors and potential National qualifiers. This group has a 1 year financial and competition commitment, but will be billed monthly. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for specific prices. This group is by coach’s invitation only.

Lessons Program

  The lessons program is a basic introduction to the sport of diving for children age 13 and under who can successfully swim one length of the diving pool unassisted. This program teaches the basic movements of diving through instruction on the ground and in the water. Divers in this program will be hand-spotted by the instructor to ensure maximal safety on the diving boards and in the water. This class meets one day per week for one hour at a time. This class operates on a ten-week session system. All new divers (age 13 and under) will be required to take at least one session of lessons. Divers will be evaluated at the end of the session to determine their placement into our developmental program. Divers in this program will be able to attend local association level meets. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for information on the cost of this program.

Developmental Program

Bronze Group

 The bronze group is the first step towards transitioning athletes from recreational divers to competitive (JO) divers. Athletes from the lessons program who show a strong potential to succeed in the sport of diving (both physically and mentally) will be asked to join the bronze group. Divers in the bronze group will learn the foundational skills necessary to prepare them for higher level diving. The bronze groups practice two days per week for one hour at a time. Quarterly evaluations will be held to determine whether or not an athlete is ready to be moved up a group. Divers in the developmental program will be able to attend local association level meets. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for information on the cost of this program.

Silver Group

 The silver group is designed for athletes who have mastered the foundational skills learned in the bronze group. Divers in the silver group will begin learning their voluntary dives along with learning how to somersault and twist. The silver groups practice three days per week for one hour at a time. Quarterly evaluations will be held to determine whether or not an athlete is ready to be moved up a group. Divers in the developmental program will be able to attend local association level meets. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for information on the cost of this program.

Gold Group

 The gold group is considered the “pre competitive team”. Athletes in this group will begin learning a competitive dive list necessary to become a JO level diver. The gold groups practice three days per week for one and a half hours at a time. Quarterly evaluations will be held to determine whether or not an athlete is ready to be moved up a group. Divers in the developmental program will be able to attend local association level meets. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for information on the cost of this program.

High School Program

 The high school program is designed for divers ages 14 and over (or 8th – 12th grade) who are interested in training year round to better prepare for their high school diving season. All new divers to the program ages 14 and over will placed in the high school group first, and later evaluated to determine whether or not they will be placed in the developmental or JO program. The high school groups practice two days per week for one and a half hours at a time. Divers in the high school program will be able to attend local association level meets. Please consult the SCD Fee Schedule for information on the cost of this program.
